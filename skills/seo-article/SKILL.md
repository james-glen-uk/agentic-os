# SEO Article

Turn a topic (typed by hand or passed from a News Oracle card) into a
structured, keyword-aware long-form article draft.

## When to use

- A trending topic from the News page deserves a full article
- You need SEO content on a subject without starting from a blank page

## Process

1. Identify the primary keyword and 3–5 secondary keywords from the topic
   and any provided headlines.
2. Write a compelling, keyword-bearing H1 title and meta description
   (≤ 155 chars).
3. Produce a 900–1400 word markdown article: intro that hooks in two
   sentences, H2/H3 sections, short paragraphs, one bulleted list, a FAQ
   section with 3 questions, and a conclusion with a call to action.
4. Weave keywords in naturally — no stuffing. Cite the provided source
   headlines where relevant as further reading links.

## Output

A complete markdown article draft. **Draft only — never publish anywhere.**
The output is saved to the Artifact Library automatically.
